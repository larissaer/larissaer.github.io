Target Audience:

Primary audience is adults ages 18-30. Most will be students. Access to the site will be split between mobile devices and laptops. A secondary audience will be anyone interested in weather.

Persona:

Persona 1

Occupation: Mechanic
Demographics and Education: 35 years old. Mark spends most of his time working on his mechanics shop and he just loves it. He is married and him and his wife love to do things outdoors. They camp in every possible weekend and break and they plan to soon go on a year long road trip with this camper Mark is fixing in his spare time.
Technology: He is not very connected with the internet. He checks his work email and a few other things on his work computer early in the morning. He does use the internet a bit more on the weekends, to plan and check on his outdoors adventures

Persona 2

Occupation: College Student
Demographics and Education: 21 years old. She loves her major in Design and she intends to work with that in the future. She is currently in her last year of school and still lives much of a busy student life. She spends most of her time inside, but she is always concern about what to wear everyday, so she makes sure to check on the daily weather. She also eats out every weekend with her friends and takes her dog out for walks at least twice a day.
Technology: Very connected to technologies. Uses her phone and laptop daily. For things such as school and researches. She also spends most of her free time browsing on the Internet and watching things online.
